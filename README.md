BrainBoost 🧠
Stop Scrolling. Start Strengthening.

BrainBoost is a mobile application designed to replace the habit of mindless "doom-scrolling" with quick, engaging, and cognitively stimulating mini-games. Whether you have 5 or 10 minutes, BrainBoost helps you stay sharp during your free time.

🚀 Features
Diverse Game Library: Includes Sudoku, Word Games, and logic puzzles

Quick-Play Design: Sessions are designed to be fast, allowing you to jump in and out instantly.

Progress Tracking: Monitor your daily streaks and see your cognitive improvements over time.

Offline Mode: Play your favorite brain teasers without needing an internet connection.

Clean UI: Minimalist, distraction-free interface to keep the focus on the gameplay.

🎮 Games Included 
Game	Description
Sudoku	Classic 9x9 logic-based number placement puzzles.
Word Hunt	Find hidden words in a grid to expand your vocabulary.
